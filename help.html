Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?
Once your order is shipped, a tracking link will be sent to your registered email address. You can also monitor your package from your profile dashboard under "My Orders".
What is your return policy?
According to consumer rights, you can return any unused item within 14 days of delivery. The product must be in its original packaging with all tags attached.
Can I change or cancel my order?
You can cancel your order within 1 hour of purchase before it prepares for shipment. Please contact our support line immediately with your order number for quick changes.
Do you offer free shipping?
Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all domestic orders over 750 TL. For international orders, shipping fees are calculated at the checkout panel.
Are the beauty and cosmetic products authentic?
Absolutely. All beauty, skincare, and cosmetic ingredients are dermatologically tested, 100% original, and supplied directly from certified brand distributors.
